# SIM-118: MediTrip Korea × 레이첼 김 (교포 2세)

## 시나리오

Rachel Kim is a Korean-American architect visiting Korea for 10 nights with her 10-year-old daughter. While preparing for the trip, she reads about Korea's renowned medical tourism and considers a comprehensive health checkup (종합검진) — something she's heard Korean-Americans visit specifically for, since it's thorough and more affordable than US equivalents. She downloads MediTrip Korea and opens it in English. The hospital search feature works well: she finds a Gangnam health checkup clinic with English-speaking staff and same-day results. The post-treatment sightseeing itinerary suggests a Cheongdam → Apgujeong cultural walk afterward, which fits her schedule. However, the content depth for her primary purpose — heritage and architectural exploration of Jeonju — is almost nonexistent in the app.

---

## 사용자 여정

### 1단계: 진입
- Research for Korea trip surfaces MediTrip Korea as a recommended app for foreigners
- Opens in English — immediately finds it useful for health checkup planning
- Books a 종합검진 (comprehensive health checkup) at a Gangnam clinic for Day 2 of her trip

### 2단계: 행동
- Hospital search: filters for "health checkup," English-speaking, Gangnam → 4 results, books online
- Post-treatment sightseeing: Gangnam area walking tour with photos — she approves the itinerary
- `tourism-photos` gallery: searches for Jeonju hanok architecture photos — limited results, no deep architectural content
- Checks for child-appropriate content for her daughter (age 10) — none
- Visa checklist feature: not applicable (US passport, visa-free entry)

### 3단계: 결과
- Uses app for the Gangnam checkup + same-day neighborhood itinerary — genuine value delivered
- Does not use app for Jeonju / heritage portion of the trip (returns to English travel blogs)
- Recommends to Korean-American friends specifically for health checkup planning
