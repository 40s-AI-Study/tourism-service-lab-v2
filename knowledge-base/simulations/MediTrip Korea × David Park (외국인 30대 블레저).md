# SIM-115: MediTrip Korea × David Park (외국인 30대 블레저)

## 시나리오

David Park comes across MediTrip Korea on Reddit r/koreatravel where someone recommends it for foreign visitors to Korea. He downloads it primarily to assess it as a PM, and secondarily because he's heard about Korea's renowned dermatology and dental clinics — he's been considering a dental checkup during his next Seoul trip. The app opens in English cleanly. He explores the hospital search feature, finds high-rated dental clinics in Gangnam with English-speaking staff, and then notices the "post-treatment sightseeing" itinerary feature. As a Bleisure traveler who wants to maximize every free afternoon, the idea of combining a dental appointment with a Gangnam neighborhood itinerary genuinely appeals to him.

---

## 사용자 여정

### 1단계: 진입
- Reddit r/koreatravel recommendation leads to download
- Opens in English — clean, navigable UI
- Scans features: hospital search, visa guide, sightseeing courses, photo gallery

### 2단계: 행동
- Explores dental clinic search: filters for English-speaking staff in Gangnam → finds 3 relevant clinics with ratings and contact info
- Checks "post-treatment sightseeing" tab: Gangnam → Cheongdam → Apgujeong coffee and gallery walk itinerary
- Notes the `area-tourism-demand-density` crowd avoidance feature — useful for his tight schedule
- Looks for audio guides for historical sites (Gyeongju, Gyeongbokgung) — limited to English, not the deep content he wants
- Time-based itinerary ("3-hour course") is absent — his key need for bleisure afternoons

### 3단계: 결과
- Keeps the app. Books a dental consultation through the app for his next trip
- Uses it alongside Google Maps for general navigation
- Wishes for deeper English historical audio guide content
