# SIM-119: MediTrip Korea × 아미나 사이드 (말레이시아 무슬림)

## 시나리오

Amina is a Malaysian Muslim digital marketer and travel influencer who is planning a 5-night Korea trip with two friends. She has been considering trying Korea's famous dermatology clinics — laser treatments and skin boosters are significantly more affordable in Korea than in Malaysia. She finds MediTrip Korea through a Korean tourism board Instagram post targeting Southeast Asian visitors. She opens the app in English, which works well. The dermatology clinic search returns several certified clinics in Gangnam with English-speaking staff. However, as a Muslim traveler, her immediate concern is whether there are halal food options and a prayer room (musalla) near the clinic. The app has no halal filter and no prayer room locator — a significant gap for the Muslim Southeast Asian market.

---

## 사용자 여정

### 1단계: 진입
- Korean tourism board Instagram post targets Southeast Asian visitors → App Store download
- Opens in English — scans features: hospital search, visa guide, sightseeing courses
- Immediately searches for dermatology clinics: finds 5 results in Gangnam with English-speaking staff

### 2단계: 행동
- Clinic detail pages: treatment menu, price range, before/after photos, English contact — impressed
- Checks "post-treatment sightseeing" for Gangnam area: coffee shops, Cheongdam walk — visually appealing
- Searches for halal restaurant filter — does not exist
- Searches for musalla / prayer room near Gangnam clinic — does not exist
- Checks if any Muslim-friendly tagging on restaurants — none found
- Growing concern: "I can book the clinic but I don't know where to eat or pray nearby"

### 3단계: 결과
- Books a consultation through the app (genuine value delivered on the medical side)
- For food and prayer logistics, falls back to HalalTrip and her Malaysian Muslim Korea travel WhatsApp group
- Posts on Instagram: "Booked my skin clinic through MediTrip Korea — works great for that! But no halal food or prayer info, Muslim travelers take note"

## Pass/Fail 판정

> **✅ PASS**

Amina Said는 MediTrip Korea의 외국인 의료관광객 타깃에 해당하며, 피부과 예약 기능이 그녀의 실제 니즈를 충족한다. 다만 할랄 음식 필터와 기도실 정보가 없어 무슬림 여행자로서의 완전한 여정 지원은 이루어지지 않는다. 종합 20점으로 PASS이나, 무슬림 사용자 완전 지원을 위한 개선 여지가 명확하다.

## 개선 제안

1. 관광 코스 탭에 할랄 인증 식당 필터와 인근 무살라 위치 표시 기능을 추가하면 말레이시아·인도네시아·중동 의료관광객 시장을 대거 흡수 가능 — 이 시장은 한국 의료관광 고성장 세그먼트
